I am Dani ! Ph.D. Student in Artificial Intelligence at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, in joint affiliation between the Barcelona Supercomputing Center (Artificial Intelligence Institute) and the Barcelona Institute for Research in Biomedicine (Structural Bioinformatics & Network Biology). Because apparently I still have some free time, I am also pursuing a M.Sc. in Bioinformatics at the University of Barcelona in parallel. I am supported by the BSC AI4Science Fellowship, a talent programme at the convergence of High Performance Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

My current research lies at the intersection of deep learning, bioinformatics, and interpretability. I focus on understanding how foundational models learn from different biological representations, and what information they encode in their latent spaces. Some of the questions that drive me are:
Can we build algorithms that model the biological complexities of our world?
Can we use them for biological and biomedical scientific discovery?
